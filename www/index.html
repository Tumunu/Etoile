Etoile: An app to help the deaf talk

Etoile has been developed to enable the hearing impaired to have real-time text conversations using the iPhone.

Putting the hearing impaired on the same level playing field as hearing people is the primary goal of Etoile. It's not a disability to be deaf when you are communicating with Etoile.

Etoile is a simple product designed to assist you. If you have an idea or an improvement that you feel others will benefit, please do not hessitate to contact us at etoile@makethingstalk.co.nz

Keeping it simple

Need help to talk with cashiers, waiters or even your friend across the room?

Tired of having your phone taken off you when you try to communicate with hearing people?

Etoile provides two simple interfaces to help you get your message across.

Text big, text small

The main Etoile interface is a flexible note pad that allows you create your message in a font size guaranteed keep your iPhone in your hands.

Moving messages

A handy feature we thought you might like is the ability to message your friends from across the room. Stand back and hit the scroll button!
